Learn from Masters

Posted on 2018-03-08 |

Mr Owen Ho(胡志健先生)

During the lunch break, I accidentally clicked on the performance delivered by Mr Owen Ho last year in Beijing concert hall. I was quite impressed by his playing of Libertango and Threnody from Gordon Jacob’s FIVE PIECES. I can still remember last year, when i was in exchange in Tsinghua University. And i was really lucky that i can be the MC in the his sharing session. During the sharing, he said “I think harmonica is the kind of instrument that you can play without looking at it.” Hearing his Libertango, I can truly feel that by closing eyes, he can concentrate more on the notes by the melody. His hand movement is also delicate, because some of his slight vibrating adds more variance to the sound(really beauitful vibrato).
